About AIPPM

The All India Political Parties Meet (AIPPM) is a unique simulation that brings together representatives from various political parties across India to deliberate on pressing national issues and policy matters.

Unlike traditional MUN committees that focus on international relations, AIPPM provides delegates with an opportunity to understand the complexities of Indian domestic politics, coalition building, and the parliamentary democratic process.

Delegates will represent different political parties, each with their distinct ideologies, vote banks, and regional interests. The committee aims to foster understanding of India's diverse political landscape and the art of consensus building in a multi-party democracy.

Key Features

  • Multi-Party Dynamics: Experience the complexity of coalition politics
  • Regional Representation: Understand diverse state and regional interests
  • Policy Formulation: Draft comprehensive national policies
  • Parliamentary Procedures: Learn Indian legislative processes
