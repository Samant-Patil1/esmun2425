About DISEC

The First Committee (Disarmament and International Security) deals with disarmament, global challenges and threats to peace that affect the international community and seeks out solutions to the challenges in the international security regime.

DISEC addresses the whole range of armament and disarmament issues, from weapons of mass destruction to conventional arms, including issues related to international security. The committee considers all disarmament and international security matters included in the UN Charter or affecting the functions of any other organ of the United Nations.

In ESMUN's DISEC simulation, delegates will tackle 21st-century security challenges including cyber warfare, space militarization, emerging technologies, and traditional disarmament issues. The committee requires analytical thinking and innovative approaches to complex security dilemmas.
