ESMUN 2025 Rules and Regulations

Dear Delegates, Greetings from EuroSchool HSR!

Please go through the detailed Rules and Regulations to be followed during ESMUN 2025.

📝

Registration Process

  • Registrations shall be made on a first-come, first-served basis. Allotments will be given considering preference and experience.
  • Ensure that the phone number submitted at the time of registration is a WhatsApp number.
  • A valid ID proof (school ID, Aadhar Card, etc.) must be submitted at the time of registration. The photo must be clear and visible.
👔

Dress Code

  • The dress code is Western formal. Indian formals are allowed for the AIPPM.
  • No skirts, shorts, or sleeveless wear are allowed for girls.
  • No school uniforms or emblems can be shown during the conference.
  • Professional Appearance: Clothes should be neat, clean, and well-fitted.
  • Footwear: Closed-toe dress shoes or formal flats are appropriate.
  • Grooming: Maintain a well-groomed appearance. Hair should be neatly styled.
⏰

Committee Rules

  • Reporting Time: Delegates must report to the campus between 7:30 AM and 8:15 AM.
  • Any participant failing to arrive on time must explain their tardiness to the EB members.
  • ID Cards: Delegates are required to wear the ID cards given to them on ALL days of the conference at all times on campus. Failure to do so will result in disqualification.
  • The decision of the Executive Board Members is final.
🚫

Anti-Harassment Policy

  • Delegates are requested to follow the anti-harassment policy at all times within the campus.
  • No vulgarity or inappropriate language/gestures will be tolerated.
  • No abusive language or slurs against other delegates, logistic members, organizers, teachers, or executive board members will be tolerated.
  • Racial, religious, and/or sexual comments will lead to immediate disqualification.
📱

Communication & Conduct

  • Messaging: Unnecessary messaging will result in warnings, followed by disqualification.
  • Cyberbullying: Cyberbullying of ANY kind will result in immediate action.
  • Bloc Formation/Voting: Delegates found exercising undue influence shall be immediately disqualified.
🍽️

Food & Beverages

  • No outside food is allowed.
  • STRICTLY NO non-veg, soft drinks, or any other beverages are allowed inside the campus.
⚠️

Reporting Issues

  • Providing Proof: Suitable proof must be provided to corroborate any complaint.
  • Reporting: Complaints must be made through the teacher responsible for that particular participant or delegation.
📢

Important Notice

Any violation of the aforementioned rules and regulations will result in immediate disqualification. No refunds will be issued under any circumstances.

Frequently Asked Questions

Western formal attire is required. Indian formals are allowed for AIPPM. No skirts, shorts, or sleeveless wear for girls. No school uniforms or emblems allowed.

No refunds will be issued under any circumstances. Any violation of rules and regulations will result in immediate disqualification without refund.
