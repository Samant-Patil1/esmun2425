Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need for student registration?

You'll need a valid student ID card, fee receipt, or enrollment certificate from your educational institution.

Can I change my committee preference after registration?

Committee changes are subject to availability and must be requested within 7 days of registration.

What is the refund policy?

Full refunds are available up to 20 days before the conference. After that, a 50% refund is applicable.

Are there group discounts available?

Yes! Groups of 5 or more from the same institution get a 10% discount. Contact us for group registration.

What should I bring to the conference?

Formal attire, notebook, pen, laptop (optional), and your registration confirmation email.
