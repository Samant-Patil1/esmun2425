Secretary General's Message

ESMUN 2025 | HSR

Dear Delegates and Faculty Advisors,

It is my pleasure to invite you to Euroschool HSR's premier Model UN conference which will be held at Euroschool HSR the 24th and 25th of July. I am Aaryan Patil, a Journalism and Mass Communications student at Manipal Academy of Higher Edition and an alumni of Euroschool HSR.

I've eagerly awaited this conference and it is the result of a 2 year trail and error stage, and now with it finally in action it's given me a sense of relief. The aim of this conference is to instill the spirit of diplomacy and nurture conflict resolution amongst the younger generation.

At ESMUN we plan to emulate a total of 5 committees with a great Executive Board, these committees will tackle complex agendas and put the delegates capabilities to the test. With topics ranging from Press Censorship to the Srebrenica Massacre, the delegates are bound to have an enriching learning experience.

"All war is the failure of diplomacy"

As the world goes through a tough time and diplomats struggle to find answers, delegates are expected to find answers to some of the most complex questions. The expectations have been set, all that's left is to let your voice be heard.

That being said I wish you all the very best and I hope to see you at the premier edition of ESMUN-2025, HSR!

Aaryan Patil

Secretary General
