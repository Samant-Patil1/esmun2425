About CCC

The Crisis Control Committee is an innovative and fast-paced committee that simulates real-time crisis scenarios requiring immediate diplomatic intervention, strategic thinking, and coordinated international response.

Unlike traditional MUN committees, CCC operates in a dynamic environment where the situation evolves continuously throughout the conference. Delegates must adapt quickly to breaking news, unexpected developments, and emerging threats while maintaining their character's core interests and objectives.

The committee features real-time updates, press conferences, emergency sessions, and backroom negotiations. Delegates may represent government officials, international organization leaders, NGO heads, or key stakeholders directly involved in crisis management and resolution.

Key Features

  • Real-Time Updates: Continuous crisis developments and breaking news
  • Dynamic Scenarios: Evolving situations requiring adaptive strategies
  • Multiple Stakeholders: Diverse roles beyond traditional country representation
  • Media Component: Press releases, interviews, and public statements
